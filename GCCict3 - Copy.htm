If you need to escalate a call, follow the guidance in the document 'Call Escalation for Contract Monitoring Team' which can be found in the downloads section on Staffnet

How to get access to ICT Training

General ICT training is provided by GEL and covers courses such as:

  • Microsoft Word 2007 (different expertise levels)
  • Microsoft Excel 2007 (different expertise levels)
  • Microsoft Outlook
  • Microsoft Project
  • Microsoft Publisher
  • Microsoft PowerPoint
  • IT Awareness
  • IT Tips & Tricks

Follow this link for more details.

If you are required to use more specific systems for your role at GCC then your Line Manager will arrange for training with the specific training team. Your Line Manager will also arrange for your access to the system.

Information handling

Without information the organisation would not be able to function so please remember to take the time out to manage the council’s information properly. It could make the difference between the council making the right and wrong decision.

You must understand your responsibilities surrounding the information that you have been given access to.
