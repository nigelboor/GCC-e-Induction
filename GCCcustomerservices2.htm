Customer Service

The Customer Services Team at Gloucestershire County Council

On average our monthly contacts split between phone, emails, letters, web based forms and face to face visits is approximately 20 000. This contact is split between members of the public and internal and external colleagues.

More information about the services we support can be reviewed on the staffnet page Customer Services Team

Some other corporate responsibilities we have are to promote the Customer Care Standards, support the Corporate Complaints Process and Implement the Customer Transformation Programme.

The Customer Care Standards can be viewed here Customer Care Standards Information under section two. This can be reviewed once you click on the link to the Communications Handbook at the bottom of the page. This also has information on our complaints process and the feedback routes available to residents in the County.

So in practical terms what exactly do we do?

The Children and Families Helpdesk deal with calls and correspondence relating to social care referrals from parents, families and professionals requiring guidance, information and advice.
Opening Times: 8am to 5pm Monday to Friday excluding bank holidays
Telephone: 01452 426565 or e-mail childrenshelpdesk@gloucestershire.gov.uk

The Adult Social Care Helpdesk offers a wide range of information and advice regarding adult social care, including referrals taken for occupational therapy and personal care.
Opening Times: 8am to 5pm Monday to Friday excluding bank holidays
Telephone 01452 426868 or e-mail socialcare.enq@gloucestershire.gov.uk

The Council Direct Team deal with calls and correspondence for a number of council services such as Libraries, Waste Management, Environment, Council Direct, Education, Registration Service, GUS helpline and overflow calls from main switchboard.
Opening Times: 8am to 5pm Monday to Friday excluding bank holidays
Contact details for services

Shire Hall Reception: 01452 425000
Opening: Monday to Friday 8.30am to 5.00pm
