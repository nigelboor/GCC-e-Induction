Security

It is your responsibility to ensure that council information is kept secure, so please remember:

  • Lock doors and windows when leaving the office;
  • Don’t let unauthorised visitors onto Council premises;
  • Keep your password secure; don’t share it;
  • Use the screen lock whenever you leave your computer (hold down the Ctrl, Alt & Delete keys at the same time, then click on Lock Computer OR hold down the Windows key & the letter L at the same time);
  • Use confidential waste facilities to destroy information;
  • Keep information secure when not on Council premises;
  • Do not leave files in public places;
  • Do not put personal information onto unencrypted devices, e.g. laptops, memory sticks, CDs;
  • Be aware of who can hear your telephone conversations;
  • Don’t access information that you don’t need to do your job;
  • Don’t use unauthorised non-GCC equipment, e.g. home computers when using the approved remote access or personal email accounts.

Please note this list is not exhaustive and you are responsible for ensuring you keep up-to-date with the Council’s security policies.
