Customer Service

The Customer Services Team provides a corporate support function to a number of services at Gloucestershire County Council. The team is currently based in Shire Hall and consists of: Adult Social Care Helpdesk, Children & Families Helpdesk, Council Direct, Shire Hall reception team, switchboard and the Village & Community Agents.

Take a look at the two videos below. They are examples of the type of calls that Customer Services deal with on a daily basis. The first is a call to the Adult Social Care Helpdesk from a family requesting assessment for support. The second is a call to Council Direct where a customer is disgruntled because he has been turned way from the Household Recycling Centre because he had a van.

The videos are an actor’s representation of the type of calls we get are there to give a flavour of the calls and should not be reflected on as a totally accurate representation.
