Security breaches

So, what if something goes wrong? It is really important that you let somebody know. The council needs to control and fix the breach as well as ensuring that it doesn’t happen again, but we don’t know where the problems are if we don’t hear about them.

A breach happens when information is deliberately or accidentally lost, wrongly used or destroyed, and/or stolen.

Examples include:

  • Damage to equipment, facilities or utilities;
  • Loss or misplacement of media (e.g. disks, tapes, paper) containing personal data or PROTECT or RESTRICTED information;
  • Virus and malicious attack;
  • Unauthorised access or attempted unauthorised access to information or ICT resources.

Details of who to inform are on staffnet .
